Privacy Policy

How we handle your data — clearly and honestly.

📅 Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Apparatus Dev ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application G1 Practice Test Ontario (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information such as name, email address, or phone number.

b) Automatically Collected Information

When you use the App, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device type and model
  • Operating system version
  • App usage data (such as screens viewed and interactions)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)

This information is used only to improve the App and display ads.

2. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob, a third-party advertising service provided by Google Inc.

Google AdMob may collect and use information such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • Cookies
  • Advertising ID
  • Usage data

This data is used to:

  • Display personalized or non-personalized ads
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

You can learn how Google uses data from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used to:

  • Operate and maintain the App
  • Improve user experience
  • Show advertisements
  • Monitor performance and stability
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent users' personal data.

Information may be shared only with trusted third-party services used by the App, such as:

  • Google AdMob

These third parties have their own privacy policies and are responsible for how they handle data.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information collected through the App. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. User Choices and Opt-Out

Users can:

  • Reset or limit ad personalization through their device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ads Settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:
