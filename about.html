About G1 Practice Test Ontario

An independent educational app to help Ontario drivers prepare for the G1 knowledge test — built on the official Driver's Handbook.

Why We Built This App

Preparing for the Ontario G1 knowledge test can be stressful — especially when you're not sure which resources to trust. We built G1 Practice Test Ontario to give every driver a simple, reliable, and free way to study for the real exam.

All study content and road rules are based on the official Ontario Driver's Handbook published by the Government of Ontario. No subscriptions, no distractions — just practice.

  • Study all G1 topics from the official handbook
  • Learn road signs with detailed explanations
  • Take realistic mock tests (40 questions each)
  • Track your progress across all topics
  • Completely free — no hidden fees or subscriptions
  • Works offline — study anywhere, anytime

Disclaimer: This app is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or representing any government entity or agency. G1 Practice Test Ontario is an independent educational app. Official source: ontario.ca/document/official-mto-drivers-handbook
